Life Cycle of an Idea¶

  1. Individual Exploratory work
  2. Collaborative Development
  3. Publication & Communication (reproducibly)
  4. Production
  5. Education

Typically :

  1. Individual Exploratory work : Matlab, R, Python, ...
  2. Collaborative Development : email, git, ...
  3. Publication & Communication (reproducibly) : pdf, github, ...
  4. Production : C, C++, Fortran, Hadoop, ...
  5. Education : A good old fashion PowerPoint ! (or ssmpg website)

But Jupyter can help for each step !
